The Problem

We are visual creatures living in a 3D world. Yet we stick to 2D media for expressing ideas.

The Solution

DynaDio rekindles our childhood instinct to draw in air. It is an entirely new way to think, create, and communicate.

Humans will be able to physically mark in 3D for the first time, revolutionising STEM education and research, design and architecture, and much more.
